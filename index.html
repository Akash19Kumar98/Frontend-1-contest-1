BNC POWER PROJECTS.LTD

About

We Empower business through Innovation and Partner through trust! BNC Power is a fast progressing and reliable entity that offers specialized and end-to-end solutions in Turnkey projects for construction of EHV Transmission lines, EHV Substations, HTLS re-stringing projects, OPGW projects, Capacitor Bank Projects. Established in the year 1967, under the banner of M/S B.N.Chaudhari Engineers and Contractors and converted into a Public limited company later on named as BNC Power Projects Ltd. Our skilled and certified professionals at BNC Power have empowered us to deliver Turnkey Construction, Maintenance of EHV lines and Substations, Capacitor Bank, Testing & Commissioning of EHV (extra high voltage) Substation, emergency restoration by using ERS System, installation of emergency restoration system and execution solutions. We use advanced tools and devices to execute customized power solutions and services with safe techniques. Our strength lies in working around the value chain to deliver on-time, seamless result adhering to the best industry practices in terms of safety guidelines.
